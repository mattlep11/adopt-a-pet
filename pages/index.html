Ready to find your new best friend?

Welcome to dogs and cats. The best adoption centre to find local rescues.

Our mission is to ensure that all strays and rescues find a loving and deserving home. We offer services both to connect animals with new families as well as rehoming those whose families no longer want them. We do this by connecting those looking to adopt to those looking to rehome and by taking in strays and rescues to our local facilities.
